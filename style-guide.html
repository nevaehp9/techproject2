Local Band Support Style Guide

This guide shows the look and feel for the local band discovery app.

Color Palette

Use a dark background with bright purple highlights and soft neon accents.

Primary: #7d4bf5

Secondary: #120b1f

Typography

Headings should be bold and easy to scan, with lighter body text for descriptions.

Example Heading

This is regular paragraph text. Keep the app easy to read with good contrast and a music-inspired style.

Buttons

Use a bright button color for primary actions like browsing bands or opening details.

Primary Button

Cards

Use cards for each band in the collection. Show the name, genre, short info, and location.
